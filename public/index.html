Use your latin keyboard to get japanese hiragana :

The "romazi" (latin transcription) to be used is the nihonshiki as shown in the following gojūon table:
a ka sa ta na ha ma ya ra wa n
i ki si ti ni hi mi ri
u ku su tu nu hu mu yu ru
e ke se te ne he me re
o ko so to no ho mo yo ro wo
To mark syllable's end when necessary, use ': kanen gives かねん while kan'en gives かんえん
